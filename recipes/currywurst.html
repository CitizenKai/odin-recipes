Description

Currywurst is one of the most iconic and oldest fast food dishes in Germany. Two regions in particular are known for their currywurst: Berlin and the Ruhr area. The two versions differ in a few details. In Berlin, traditionally sausages without casings are used and the sauce is quite thick. In the Ruhr area, normal bratwurst sausages are grilled and the sauce is more thin. In my recipe, I combine the best of both worlds.

Prep Time Cooking Time Total Time
5 mins 10 mins 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cola
  • 5 tbsp. apple sauce
  • 2 tbsp. curry powder
  • 1 ts. tabasco
  • 2 tbsp. worcestershire sauce
  • 2 ts. Lime juice
  • 1½ cup ketchup
  • salt

Steps

  1. Boil down the cola to about ½ cup.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients, season with salt to taste, and bring to a gentle boil once more.
  3. Done
