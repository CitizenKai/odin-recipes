Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Roman pasta dish. This traditional recipe by Vincenzo's Plate is made with eggs, not cream!

Prep Time Cooking Time Total Time
5 mins 15 mins 20 mins

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Salt (rock salt)
  • 10 oz. spaghetti (alternatively spaghettoni, rigatoni, or Paccheri)
  • 5 oz. guanciale (alternatively pig cheek or panchetta)
  • 2 cups of pecorino romano cheese, grated (alternatively parmesan cheese if pecorino is too strong for you or you can't get it)
  • 4 eggs
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Spaghetti carbonara takes just a short time to make so first up, bring salted water in a large pot to a boil to cook your pasta.
  2. Cook the pasta according to the instructions on the packaging, making sure the pasta is al dente. Do not overcook the pasta!
  3. Cut the skin off guanciale (making sure to leave the peppery/seasoned crust), then Cut it into cubes.
  4. Put a large frying pan on the stove at a low to medium heat. For the spaghett carbonara recipe to be just right, add the guanciale into the pan, let it cook very slowly and it will render out all the delicious fat.
  5. Let the guanciale slowly simmer and crisp up very gently.
  6. Get your mixing bowl and add 4 eggs, then whisk them really well.
  7. Next, add the pecorino cheese and lots of pepper berfor mixing it together really well. This will create a scrumptious cream for you to add to your pasta.
  8. Once the pasta is ready, using a set of tongs, take out the pasta from the boiling water and add it straight to the pan.
  9. Turn off the stove, so the pasta and guanciale stop cooking.
  10. Next, using the ladle, get a full scoop of pasta water out of the pot and add it to the egg and cheese cream, then mix through well using a fork.
  11. Now, turn the stove back on a low heat, add some pasta water to the pan, and now, pour the cream over the top, then mix everything with your tongs - be gentle, but be fast! Let it cook through but don't let the eggs scramble or you will end up with a frittata!
  12. Keep mixing the cream until it just starts to thicken, stir and then serve.

Buon appetito
